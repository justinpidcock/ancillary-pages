TeamStyle Tips

for Happy Apparel

Apparel that is well-cared-for leads to you and your team creating a great first impression. Follow these simple steps to keep your TeamStyle looking sharp.

Never Iron Anything

  1. Wash according to instructions.
  2. Toss in the dryer for 10 minutes.
  3. Shake out your shirt and hang it up on a hanger to fully dry.
  4. Need a wrinkle refresh? Just pop your shirt in the dryer for 5 minutes.

Easily Defeat A Stain

  1. Lay the garment out flat and apply a few drops of Dawn dish detergent.
  2. For tough stains, rub under running water.
  3. Wash normally with your other items.
  4. Before drying, check the stain. If it is still there, repeat steps 1-3.
  5. Wear your clean shirt with pride!

A Better Sweater

  1. Frequent washing will remove pills and keep your sweater looking sharp.
  2. Dry your sweater on medium heat for 10 minutes to release wrinkles and lay flat to dry.
