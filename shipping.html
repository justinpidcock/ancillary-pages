Shipping

We strive to process orders as quickly as we possibly can. Most orders placed Monday through Friday are usually shipped within 24 hours. If your order is placed after 2:30 PM EST, the earliest it will ship is the next business day. Orders placed over the weekend (Friday after 2:30 PM - Sunday) ship the following Monday.


We offer the following shipping methods:

SHIPPING METHOD ESTIMATED DELIVERY
FedEx Ground 2-5 business days. Deliveries are made Mon-Fri.
FedEx 2 Day 2 days if ordered by 2:30 PM EST. No Saturday delivery.
FedEx Priority Next Day if ordered by 2:30 PM EST. No Saturday delivery.
